# Code of Conduct

AI Economy Ledger is committed to a respectful, evidence-led, and inclusive contributor community.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and respectful.
- Separate factual evidence from interpretation and disclose uncertainty.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of experience.
- Credit contributors and sources accurately.
- Disclose material conflicts of interest relevant to data or analysis.
- Protect private information and report security concerns privately.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, fabricated evidence, undisclosed promotional manipulation, deliberate data poisoning, or publishing another person's private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. The maintainer may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants depending on severity and pattern.

Enforcement decisions should protect affected people, preserve relevant evidence, minimize unnecessary disclosure, and document material governance lessons without exposing private details.
